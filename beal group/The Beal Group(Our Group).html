Our Group

The Beal Group of Companies: A family controlled business, passionate about its products & service levels and large enough to cope with all requirements.

Rhino Defence brings together the best of both worlds: Rhino-UK and Tony Beal Ltd, both part of The Beal Group, who have a long tradition of manufacturing quality approved finished textile based items for the UK & International defence market. Serving the armed forces for more than 25 years.

https://rhino-defence.co.uk/

Graffix Detail was established in 1998 and acquired by The Beal Group in November 2014. We now have an ability to print with both small and large format screen printing and we also have super wide format digital printing capability. This, combined with our 60,000 sq ft group manufacturing facility means we have the ability to print and manufacture large sized and large volume products.

https://www.graffixdetail.co.uk/

Rhino UK is a manufacturing and sales facility acquired by The Beal Group in September 2013 to service key markets in the West Midlands. Core products and services offered match those offered by Tony Beal Ltd in Scotland, with the aim that both companies support each other for the benefit of our customers and the industrial textile market as a whole.

https://www.rhino-uk.co.uk//

Tony Beal Ltd is a manufacturer of commercial textile products for a number of industries, including oil & gas, defence, modular, construction, airmotive, automotive, transportation, leisure, agriculture, engineering & construction. Located in Glasgow with over 70 employees, we have a fleet of sales vans which cover most of mainland Britain.

https://www.tonybeal.com//
