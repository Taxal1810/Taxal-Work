Meet the holy grail of cryptography

The power of FHE is unmatched. It’s a cutting-edge mathematical encryption scheme that facilitates computations on encrypted data without the need for decryption!

This enables deployment of confidential smart contracts, ensuring that transaction inputs and states stay encrypted throughout the entire computation process.
