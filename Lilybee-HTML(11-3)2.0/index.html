Relief For Sports
Related Injuries

MAGNESIUM relaxes your muscles and controls their contractions. It helps lessen the build-up of lactic acid, which can cause muscular tension. This then enables your muscles to get the oxygen they need. Magnesium removes the calcium buildup which causes muscle strain and resets the muscle to its original state.

Essential Oils

Pure essential oils aid body and mind in its proper function and wellness. They provide a wide range of extremely therapeutic, anti-inflammatory, regenerating and skin-supporting effects and are known for their antioxidant properties.

Mineral Compounds

Minerals create hundreds of biochemical reactions in your body which regulate cellular regeneration and repair the skin, have high anti-inflammatory properties, may help with deep and restful sleep and can aid muscle recovery.

Marine Botanicals

The sea moss, marine algae and seaweed in particular promote glowing skin and have highly therapeutic and anti-inflammatory properties due to the high concentration of zinc, vitamin a, and sulphur.
