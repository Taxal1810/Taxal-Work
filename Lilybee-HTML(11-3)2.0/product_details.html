This skin polish uses bamboo powder to gently exfoliate the skin and remove impurities. Infused with Moringa oil and Pomegranate seed oil to nourish and hydrate.

Product Details

Properties & Benefits

Moringa oil - Rich in Omega 9 that conditions and nourishes the skin, especially dry and mature skin types. Penetrates and softens the skin without feeling greasy.

Pomegranite seed oil - A hydrating and soothing oil rich in omega-5 and ellagic acid. This versatile oil is a potent natural antioxidant offering several benefits for all skin types.

Neroli Hydrolate - Tones the skin, calms and relaxes the senses.

Bamboo exfoliant powder - This microdermabrasion-grade exfoliant is suitable for the face or the body and is gentle enough to use every day.

Vitamin E - An extremely effective antioxidant that helps with dark spots, inflammation and hydration.

Lactic acid - An exfoliating alpha-hydroxy acid that helps promote the shedding of dead skin cells. A milder alternative to using Glycolic Acid, it is less irritating whilst being just as effective.
