What does vitamin E do to the skin?

By applying vitamin E to your skin helps protect delicate skin from damage due to its antioxidant properties. It’s also extremely hydrating – it’s both a ‘humectant’ and an ‘emollient’ – so it helps your skin absorb water, cream etc that helps trap it within the skin.
